Paidi Rakesh Reddy

MLA, Armur Constituency, Telangana

About & Early Life

Date of Birth: 20 March 1967 | Ankapur, Nizamabad

Father: Paidi Ganga Reddy

Party: Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

Constituency: Armur, Telangana Legislative Assembly

Paidi Rakesh Reddy's life is an inspiring story of resilience and self-made success. Born in Ankapur, Nizamabad district, he had to discontinue his education after Class VIII. In 1987, at the age of 20, he moved to the United Arab Emirates in search of a better livelihood — starting as a construction labourer and later working as a driver. Through sheer determination and hard work, he rose from these humble beginnings to become a successful entrepreneur.

In 1994, Rakesh Reddy returned to India and channelled his experience and earnings into building businesses in red sanders exports, real estate, and healthcare. His success in the private sector gave him the means and motivation to give back to his community. He founded the Rakesh Reddy Foundation, a non-profit organisation dedicated to community service in the Armur–Nizamabad region, providing healthcare, education, and infrastructure support to underserved families.

On June 1, 2023, he formally joined the Bharatiya Janata Party in the presence of national leader Tarun Chugh. In the 2023 Telangana Legislative Assembly elections, he contested from the Armur constituency and won by a record margin of 29,669 votes over Congress candidate Prodduturi Vinay Kumar Reddy — one of the biggest victories in the region's electoral history.

Political Journey & Career Timeline

1987 — UAE Journey Begins

At age 20, moved to the United Arab Emirates. Started as a construction labourer and later worked as a driver — the foundation of a life-changing journey of perseverance.

1994 — Return to India & Entrepreneurship

Returned to India and established successful businesses in red sanders exports, real estate, and healthcare. Built financial strength to serve his community.

Pre-2023 — Social Service & Foundation Work

Founded the Rakesh Reddy Foundation, a non-profit providing healthcare camps, educational support, and community development in the Armur–Nizamabad area.

1 June 2023 — Joins BJP

Formally joined the Bharatiya Janata Party in the presence of national leader Tarun Chugh, beginning his mainstream political career.

December 2023 — Wins Armur MLA Seat

Won the 2023 Telangana Legislative Assembly election from Armur, defeating Congress candidate Prodduturi Vinay Kumar Reddy by a record 29,669 votes.

January 2024 — Malkajgiri In-charge

Appointed as in-charge for the Malkajgiri Lok Sabha constituency by the BJP, expanding his leadership role within the party.

February 2024 — BJP Assembly Treasurer

Appointed as BJP Legislative Assembly Treasurer — a mark of the party's trust and confidence in his financial discipline and commitment.

2024 Onwards — Development Work in Armur

Focused on delivering on election promises: improving local roads, water supply, and civic infrastructure across the Armur constituency.

Achievements & Vision for Armur

Community Engagement

Shri Rakesh Reddy is renowned for his grassroots connection with constituents. Through regular meetings, field visits, and the Rakesh Reddy Foundation, he ensures the voice of every citizen is heard and acted upon.

Infrastructure Development

Since becoming MLA, he has prioritised the development of roads, water supply, and sanitation in the Armur constituency — bringing tangible, visible progress to residents' daily lives.

Healthcare & Education

Through the Rakesh Reddy Foundation, he has funded healthcare camps, educational scholarships, and self-employment programmes for youth and women in the Nizamabad region.

Vision

Shri Paidi Rakesh Reddy envisions an Armur that is prosperous, educated, and self-reliant — where every family has access to clean water, quality roads, healthcare, and economic opportunity. His journey from a labourer in the UAE to a powerful voice in the Telangana Assembly is itself a testament to what determined service can achieve.
