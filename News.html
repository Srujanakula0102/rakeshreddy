ELECTION 2023
Historic Win — Armur Constituency, 29,669 Votes Margin

Paidi Rakesh Reddy created history in the 2023 Telangana Legislative Assembly elections by winning the Armur constituency with a record margin of 29,669 votes. He defeated Congress candidate Prodduturi Vinay Kumar Reddy, cementing his position as a dominant political force in the Nizamabad district. His victory was celebrated across Armur with enthusiasm, with supporters calling it a reflection of the people's trust in his grassroots work and his vision for development in the constituency. He is the first BJP MLA to win from Armur with such a large margin.

PARTY ROLE
Appointed BJP Assembly Treasurer & Malkajgiri In-charge

In a mark of the party's confidence in his leadership, Shri Paidi Rakesh Reddy was appointed as BJP Legislative Assembly Treasurer in February 2024, and as in-charge for the Malkajgiri Lok Sabha constituency in January 2024. These appointments expand his role beyond Armur and signal his growing stature within the BJP's Telangana unit. He has been actively participating in party meetings, state-level discussions, and public outreach programmes across Telangana.

DEVELOPMENT
Roads & Water Supply — Armur Gets Major Infrastructure Boost

True to his election promises, Shri Rakesh Reddy has been actively driving infrastructure development in the Armur constituency — focusing on road construction, water supply systems, and civic improvements. He has personally supervised multiple road repair and development projects, held public consultations with residents, and ensured timely follow-up on water supply complaints. His hands-on approach has earned praise from citizens and local media alike.

FOUNDATION
Rakesh Reddy Foundation — Healthcare & Community Camps

The Rakesh Reddy Foundation, established by Shri Paidi Rakesh Reddy, continues to conduct free healthcare camps, distribute educational materials, and support self-employment programmes for women and youth in the Nizamabad region. The Foundation has become a beacon of social welfare activity in Armur. It regularly partners with hospitals, schools, and NGOs to deliver services to communities that have traditionally lacked access to basic resources.
